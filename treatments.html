Treatment & Training

Hands-on manual therapy paired with golf-specific exercises that reduce pain, improve mobility, and build a routine your body can stick with.

Why Manual Therapy?

Manual therapy is a critical part of your recovery and golf performance. It reduces pain, improves mobility, and enhances your body's ability to move freely for an efficient, powerful swing.

Techniques

Tools of the Trade

Each treatment is customized to your body and your goals. Below are the manual therapy techniques we use.

Soft Tissue Mobilization

Breaks down muscle adhesions and improves circulation for quicker recovery.

Cupping Therapy

Gentle suction promotes blood flow, reduces tension, and accelerates healing.

IASTM / Scraping

Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization targets tight fascia and minimizes soft tissue restrictions.

Joint Mobilization

Restores normal movement patterns by improving joint mechanics.

PNF Stretching

Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation. Hands-on stretching and strengthening that enhances flexibility, coordination, and neuromuscular control.

Kinesiotaping

Therapeutic taping technique that supports muscles and joints without restricting motion.

Beyond the Table

Hands-On Plus Practice

Manual therapy resets your body in the moment, but the change doesn't hold on its own. Every program at Elite Golf PT pairs hands-on treatment with personalized golf-specific exercises designed for your body and your swing.

You'll leave each visit with targeted mobility drills, strength and stability work, and swing prep routines mapped to the limitations we screened. Work you can do at home, at the range, or before a round.

The biggest gains in rehab come from what you do between sessions. Real, lasting change happens when treatment, exercise, and consistency work together over time. There's no shortcut.

Sticking to the routine is the long-term solution. We'll build one that fits your life.
