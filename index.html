About

Dr. Jeremy Velasco

PT, DPT, CPT, TPI

Owner / Physical Therapist

Jeremy Velasco is the founder of Elite Golf Physical Therapy and a San Diego native with a passion for helping golfers feel and play their best. Jeremy combines his expertise in rehabilitation and golf biomechanics to create customized programs that keep golfers pain-free and performing at their highest level.

Jeremy has also performed treatments on professional senior women LPGA golfers, bringing the same tour-level care he delivers to elite athletes into every program he writes for Elite Golf PT clients.

When he's not helping clients improve their game, Jeremy enjoys golfing, playing tennis, running, and exploring new destinations. He's also a foodie at heart and loves discovering unique culinary spots. At home, he's often spending time with his energetic Labrador, Mango.

Jeremy's vision for Elite Golf PT is simple: to provide the luxury of top-tier, professional-level care, typically reserved for elite golfers, and make it accessible to golf enthusiasts across San Diego. His goal is to help every client heal pain, improve motion, and golf better.
