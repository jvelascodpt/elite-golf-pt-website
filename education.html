How We Assess

Two of the most respected frameworks in golf and movement, combined into a single assessment, so every plan starts with the truth about your body and your swing.

Why It Matters

Without a Real Assessment, You're Guessing.

Most golf fitness programs treat every player the same. They prescribe generic mobility drills, hope something sticks, and call it personalized. The result is wasted time, persistent pain, and the same swing problems you've been fighting for years.

A swing fault that's a hip restriction for one player is a thoracic mobility issue for another. Loss of distance might trace back to your ankles, not your shoulders. Without a screen, there's no way to know, and no way to write a plan that actually works.

That's why every Elite Golf PT program starts with a real assessment, built from two of the most respected systems in the field.

The Framework

Two Systems. One Assessment.

We're certified in both, and we use them together. Here's how each one contributes to your plan.

Framework 01

TPI Medical Level 3

The Titleist Performance Institute's certified screen for the Body-Swing Connection. The same framework used to assess Tour pros and weekend players alike. It tells us exactly what your body can and cannot do: rotation, hinge, tilt, stability, mobility, and power.

Gray Institute

Functional Golf System

Framework 02

Applied Functional Science

Gray Institute's Applied Functional Science (AFS) is the science of how the body really moves, in all three planes, as an integrated system. Through the Functional Golf System and Chain Reaction® Biomechanics, it shows us why your body is doing what it's doing in the swing, and how the kinetic chain is compensating.

The Synthesis

How They Work Together

TPI tells us what is limiting your swing. AFS tells us why it's happening, and how to fix the root cause, not the symptom.

Combining the two means we're not just identifying that you can't rotate well. We're tracing it back to a stiff ankle, a weak glute, a thoracic spine that doesn't extend, or whatever the real cause is in your kinetic chain.

Your program isn't generic golf fitness. It's the specific work your body needs to heal pain, improve motion, and golf better, built from a screen that respects both the swing and the science of how you move.
